Aiming to empower women through sustainable fashion,
sustainable fashion, inka atletika is a proudly-filipino and
conscious activwear brand that offers adventures-inspired
and purpose-driven collections.

Our products are designed to be multi-purpose because we
want to encourage slow fashion and re-waering of pleces.
We design our clothes to last and to withstand time in an
effort to reduce landfill waste.

We've always believed that it is in our hands to make a
change and we're starting that now.
